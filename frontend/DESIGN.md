## Design System

### Colors (OKLCH)
- **Primary:** `oklch(0.65 0.18 280)` (Academic Purple)
- **Neutral:** `oklch(0.98 0.005 280)` (Subtle warm off-white background)
- **Neutral Dark:** `oklch(0.12 0.005 280)` (Subtle warm deep-dark background)
- **Accent (Success):** `oklch(0.70 0.15 150)` (Verification approved)
- **Accent (Alert):** `oklch(0.75 0.20 30)` (Needs action)

### Typography
- **Primary:** System sans-serif (Inter stack).
- **Scale:** 
  - `h1`: 2rem (32px), weight 700
  - `h2`: 1.5rem (24px), weight 600
  - `body`: 1rem (16px), line-height 1.6
  - `small`: 0.875rem (14px), weight 400

### Components & Layout
- **Spacing:** 4px, 8px, 16px, 24px, 40px, 64px scale.
- **Elevation:** Substantial, low-chroma shadow for focused interaction cards (no border stripes).
- **Interactive:**
  - Standard focus ring: `2px solid oklch(0.65 0.18 280)`
  - Transition: `ease-out-quart`, 200ms
