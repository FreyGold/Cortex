## Design Context

### Users
Primary users are university students using the product during study sessions, exam prep, and collaborative coursework. Their core jobs are to organize notes quickly, retrieve relevant course material fast, and reduce cognitive load while switching between notes, resources, and AI tools.

### Brand Personality
Calm, credible, and academically focused. The interface should feel trustworthy and organized rather than flashy. Emotional goals: confidence, clarity, and momentum.

### Aesthetic Direction
Refined editorial-utility style with warm neutrals and neural-purple accents already defined in the token system. Light-first experience with strong readability and clean structural hierarchy. Avoid generic “AI dashboard” patterns and decorative effects that do not improve comprehension.

### Design Principles
1. Clarity over decoration: every visual decision should improve scanning, orientation, or action confidence.
2. Strong reading rhythm: left-aligned typography, predictable spacing cadence, and content-first layouts.
3. Fast decision flow: surface primary actions immediately and keep secondary options visible but quiet.
4. Distinctive but restrained brand: use neural-purple accents intentionally, not everywhere.
5. Bilingual quality by default: English/Arabic parity with proper RTL behavior and no untranslated key UI.
